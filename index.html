About Buzzwoo Reactive

In 2016 it was all about being active. In 2021, not so much anymore! We will bring the active culture back again, and we'll be doing it together, as a team.

Every KM will be rewarded with a donation amount (10 THB/KM Running and 3 THB/KM Cycling). The funds will be used to support Santisook Dog Rescue and Urban Light in Chiang Mai, Thailand. To stay up to date with our progress, check out this page and say "Hi" on our Facebook Page.
